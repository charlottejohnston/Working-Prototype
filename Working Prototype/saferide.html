The Way Ridesharing Should Be:
Revolutionizing transportation for Bowdoin students

Saferide (aka the Bowdoin Shuttle) provides safe and convenient transportation for Bowdoin students within a one-mile radius of campus. It is open seven days a week. An interactive map for all the Bowdoin Saferide stops can be found here.
